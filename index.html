A better way to organize your Slack team’s links and files

Sniffy monitors your team’s Slack channels and makes it easy to find every link and file shared by you and your teammates.

Never miss a link or file again

You remember that a colleague shared an interesting link or file, but you can’t find it simply because there’s just too much going on in your channels? No worries. Sniffy lists every link and file shared in all channels you joined.

Smart filters

Find links and files shared by everyone, a specific colleague or yourself. Today, this week, last month, last year. Sniffy gets it done.

Fast, convenient and clutter-free

See only the 10 most recent links and files shared in a channel or get a DM from Sniffy with a complete list of what was shared.

Save links and files for later

Got interested in a link or file, but don’t have time to check it out right away? Save it for later and easily go back to it in the future. Plus, saved links and files don’t get mixed up with starred text messages on Slack.

Coming to your browser soon

Sniffy Web helps you to be on top of every link and file that have been shared on your Slack workspace. Apply filters, sort by channels, favorite items. No more wasting time finding the stuff that matters.
